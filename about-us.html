Who We Are

Welcome to Jerusalem Heritage School

Located in Lira City, Northern Uganda, Jerusalem Heritage School is a private Christian-founded primary school dedicated to holistic, value-based education. We nurture learners with a focus on academic excellence, life skills, faith, and integrity.

We cater to pupils from Nursery through Primary Seven, integrating modern teaching methodologies with moral and spiritual growth.

Our mission is to produce learners who are creative, competent, and morally upright, prepared to lead and succeed in today's world.

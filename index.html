About Us

Get to Know Jerusalem Heritage School

Jerusalem Heritage School is a vibrant, forward-thinking primary school based in Lira, Northern Uganda. We provide high-quality pre-primary and primary education that is not only affordable and accessible but also sustainable and impactful.

Our approach integrates life skills, technology, and values-based learning to prepare learners for today’s challenges and tomorrow’s opportunities—locally and globally.

We believe every child has a unique potential. At Jerusalem Heritage School, we strive to unlock that potential through holistic, child-centered education.

Core Purpose

Mission, Mandate & Values

Our Mission

To promote high-quality pre-primary and primary educational services in a sustainable, affordable, and profitable manner throughout Uganda, starting from Northern Uganda.

Our Mandate

To produce learners equipped with practical life skills and relevant knowledge for today’s world, enabling them to compete favorably for global opportunities.
